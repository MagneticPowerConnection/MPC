New Technology

Battery pack built with MPC technology utilizes durable, large-surface, strongly pressed copper contacts with an innovative design, coated with a metallic contact layer. The exceptional durability of MPC contacts results from their design and operational nature.

02 /

Unique Connection Design

The materials used for the contacts, combined with their large contact area (20 mm²), enable the transfer of very high currents. Limited air access to the tightly pressed large contact surfaces prevents corrosion. Since the contacts can be easily cleaned (e.g., every 4–5 years during cell replacement or periodic inspection), MPC plates are expected to remain functional for many years.

03 /

Increased Safety

The flexible design, combined with strong magnetic contact pressure, allows MPC contacts to absorb shocks, making them ideal for high demanding projects.

04 /

Create Your Custom Battery Packet In Less Than 1 Minute

Fully customised, refurbishing batteries easy as never before. Rebuild the whole battery packet in less than one minute. It is possible now only with the MPC solution. Reliable and strong connection.

05 /

Advance Battery Technology Design

High flexibility resulting in excellent shock and vibration resistance. During UN38.3 vibration testing (with 8G acceleration), MPC connections outperform over welded connections. Their lightweight nature (0.2 g per contact) prevents inertia-related damage, ensuring durability even under extreme conditions.
